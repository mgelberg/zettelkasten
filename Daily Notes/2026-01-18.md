**09:07**
Well hello there. Been a minute etc etc. It's not as though I wasn't being productive this week, and I've even been opening up Obsidian. But the main focus (outside of work with Masterworks) has been the [[Jan 2026 Set]] which happened last Friday. It went great, definitely the largest crowd I've played since... Haverfest? Which obviously didn't actually count. A bit bigger than the 3 people who came out last October. It really does pay to 1) have a guy as a apparently connected as Keegan is and 2) to have dance music, or you know, any genre that people actually want to listen to.

There were parts of the night I did feel a bit sad, to be honest-- not because of anything with the music, just feeling very much that a part of my life was very much gone. So many young people, so many attractive people, with the main concern of the night being "where am I going next"? I'm not saying that I want that all the time, it's just funny to be reminded of how much my life is constrained by my child, my responsibilities.

It was like seeing a ghost, a specter of a life that I don't participate in anymore.
